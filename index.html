An all new region protection system gives you precise control of what is allowed where. Read more about the Region Module.

Moderate your Chat

Administrate your Server

VoxelGuest provides you and your players with all kinds of useful commands and tools. Vanishing, teleportation, fake-quitting, AFK player handling, querying server stats and purging entities is what the General Module is for.

VoxelGuest is the powerful user management and server administration plugin for Bukkit Minecraft servers developed by The VoxelPlugineering Team. Used by The VoxelBox, every module gets put to the acid test every day.

About

A conglomeration of modules splits VoxelGuest's functionality into easy-to-use portions. Protect regions of your world using a flexible region protection system, list online players, keep asshats away from your server, vanish, fake-quit or simply go AFK. All this and much more is made possible by our modules. Read more about VoxelGuest's modules on our documentation page.
